Overview 

The Aquera platform enables scalable, automated data management across enterprise applications. Through seamless integrations, it helps organizations eliminate manual processes, ensure consistency, and optimize workflows across complex IT infrastructures.

The ADP to D365 Finance and Operations Integration automates the transfer and synchronization of workforce data across multiple enterprise systems. It connects ADP (the HR system), Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) (the identity system), Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (the finance and operations platform), and Exflow (the invoice approval system, an add-on for Dynamics 365). The integration ensures that HR, finance, and IT systems maintain a unified and accurate view of employee, organizational, and user information.

In this integration, ADP provides employee and organizational information. This information is used in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (D365 F&O) to create and maintain employee records and organizational structures. Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) oversees user accounts and system access, and D365 synchronizes this user information with its system users. ExFlow uses D365 system user information to control access to invoice and expense approvals.

The integration runs as a scheduled synchronization process that detects changes in ADP and Microsoft Entra ID and applies the necessary updates across connected systems. When a new employee is added in ADP, a corresponding employee record is created in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations. Based on the configuration, user records from Microsoft Entra ID are synchronised into D365 as system users, and matching ExFlow user records are created to enable approval workflows.

Any updates to employee attributes such as job title, department, position, or reporting structure are automatically reflected in D365 Finance & Operations, ensuring organizational hierarchies and workforce data remain accurate. These updates are also propagated to related system user and ExFlow records where applicable, maintaining consistency across HR, identity, and finance systems.

When an employee leaves the organization, the integration processes the termination in D365 Finance & Operations and updates related system user and ExFlow records to restrict access and prevent unauthorized activity, in line with configured lifecycle rules.

In addition to employee data, the integration synchronizes job and position information from ADP into D365 Finance & Operations, ensuring that positions, reporting relationships, and organizational structures remain up to date and aligned with HR data.
