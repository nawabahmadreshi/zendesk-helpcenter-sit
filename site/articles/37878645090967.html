Overview

About this Integration

This integration manages the employee lifecycle between Dayforce and Okta. It automatically provisions new hires during onboarding, updates employee details for role changes, and securely disables accounts during offboarding. It features smart capabilities, such as automatically generating unique usernames and emails with AI, and advanced options, such as creating custom attributes in Okta and syncing data back from Okta to Dayforce.
