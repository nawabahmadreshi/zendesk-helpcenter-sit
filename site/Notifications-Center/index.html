Overview

Notifications Center allows you to create and maintain notification templates that can be utilized by integrations configured in your tenant. These templates enable integrations to send notifications through channels such as email, Microsoft Teams, and Slack. When an integration is executed, the notifications are triggered and delivered based on the configurations in the selected template.

Notifications Center serves as a centralized platform for configuring and managing various notifications, thus eliminating the need to navigate across multiple integrations. It provides a streamlined approach for maintaining all notification templates in one place, improving efficiency and ease of management.

This guide provides details on how to set up notification templates for your various integrations.

Set Up Notification Templates

This section covers details on how to set up a notification template. A single template can be configured to send notifications via email, Microsoft Teams, and Slack.

Log in to the Aquera Admin portal.

Click on your profile icon at the bottom-left corner of the portal and click Manage Account. The Manage Account page is displayed.

Navigate to the Tenant Preferences tab and click Notifications Center.
